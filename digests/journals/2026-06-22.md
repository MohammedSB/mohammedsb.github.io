### 1. [FetalCLIP: a visual-language foundation model for fetal ultrasound image analysis](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41746-026-02907-9)
*npj Digital Medicine · Jun 20, 2026*  
**Tags:** Foundation Models, Vision-Language Models, Medical Imaging

FetalCLIP is a CLIP-style vision-language foundation model trained on paired fetal ultrasound images and text, enabling zero-shot and transferable performance across fetal imaging tasks. It directly aligns with CLIP-style multimodal foundation model work, extending contrastive image-text pretraining into a new medical imaging domain.

### 2. [Myocardial Temporal-Mechanical Self-Supervision Model for Contrast-Free Myocardial Infarction Segmentation with Label-Free Training.](https://doi.org/10.1109/TMI.2026.3704460)
*IEEE Trans. Medical Imaging · Jun 17, 2026*  
**Tags:** Self-Supervised Learning, Medical Imaging, Segmentation

This work introduces a temporal-mechanical self-supervised model for contrast-free myocardial infarction segmentation that trains without labels and avoids reliance on strictly paired CINE and contrast-enhanced sequences. It is relevant for its label-free, self-supervised approach to a clinically important segmentation problem.

### 3. [FlowLet: Conditional 3D brain MRI synthesis using wavelet flow matching.](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.media.2026.104161)
*Medical Image Analysis · Jun 15, 2026*  
**Tags:** Generative Models, Medical Imaging

FlowLet uses conditional wavelet flow matching to synthesize 3D brain MRI with strong anatomical fidelity and diversity while drastically cutting the sampling steps required by diffusion models. It is a fast, high-quality generative approach to volumetric medical image synthesis.

*npj Digital Medicine · Jun 19, 2026*

### 8. [Clinical Implementation of AI for Pulmonary Embolism Detection in over 30 000 CT Pulmonary Angiography Examinations.](https://doi.org/10.1148/ryai.250017)
*Radiology: AI · Jul 2026*  
**Tags:** Clinical AI, Radiology AI, Deployment
