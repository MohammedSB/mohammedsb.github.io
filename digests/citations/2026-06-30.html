Citing: MedSAM2: Segment Anything in 3D Medical Images and Videos

Correction-aware interactive 3D tumor segmentation with sparse and revisable prompts

Hao Li, Haoxuan Li · 2026

What they say: Based on the citing contexts: MedSAM2 adapts the SAM-style promptable segmentation interface to medical data, extending it to 3D medical images and videos (rather than just 2D), though it often operates with 2D or slice-wise prompts; it is grouped among medical systems that extend promptable segmentation to volumetric tumor segmentation, and it studies human-in-the-loop behavior in this broader 3D setting.

  • MedSAM [21] and MedSAM2 [22] adapt the SAM-style interface to medical images and videos, often operating with 2D or slice-wise prompts.
  • Starting from point-and box-based prompting in SAM [12], recent medical systems extend promptable segmentation to 2D and 3D medical images, including volumetric tumor segmentation [5, 7, 10, 16, 17, 21, 22, 29].
  • MedSAM2 [22] extends promptable segmentation to 3D medical images and videos and studies human-in-the-loop behavior in this broader setting.
