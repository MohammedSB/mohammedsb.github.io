### 1. [US and Iran reach framework peace deal to end war](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2026/jun/14/trump-calls-for-restraint-israel-airstrikes-beirut-us-iran-peace-deal)
*The Guardian*

**What happened:** The US and Iran have announced a framework peace deal to end their 15-week war, declared jointly by Donald Trump and senior Iranian officials. The agreement is expected to reopen the vital Strait of Hormuz shipping route, though precise terms and timing remain unclear and are still being negotiated, with formal signing reportedly planned in Switzerland.

**Background:** The war had lasted more than 100 days, closing the Strait of Hormuz, stranding tens of thousands of seafarers, and triggering a global energy supply crisis.

**Why it matters:** A genuine end to the conflict would relieve the world's most important oil chokepoint and reshape Middle East geopolitics, but unresolved questions over sanctions relief and Iran's nuclear program leave the deal's durability in doubt.

*Middle East Eye*

**What happened:** Senior Israeli officials publicly rejected the newly announced US-Iran agreement, stating it 'does not bind' Israel in any way since Israel is not a party to it. The statements came amid fresh Israeli attacks across southern Lebanon.

**Why it matters:** Israeli rejection is the single biggest threat to the ceasefire holding, since continued Israeli operations could collapse the deal that Tehran says is conditional on Israel's adherence.
