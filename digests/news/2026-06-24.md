### 1. [IAEA chief, Trump says Iran agreed to nuclear inspections, Tehran denies deal - report](https://www.jpost.com/middle-east/iran-news/article-900265)
*The Jerusalem Post · Israeli · right-leaning*

**What happened:** Trump and the IAEA chief said Iran has agreed to allow long-term nuclear inspections following the US-Iran framework deal, but Tehran's foreign ministry flatly denied making any new commitments after the Switzerland talks. The dispute unfolded alongside friction over US naval positioning near the Strait of Hormuz.

**Background:** The claim follows a framework agreement to end the roughly four-month US-Iran war, with negotiators pushing for a final deal within 60 days and inspectors said to be invited back after Iranian nuclear sites were bombed.

**Why it matters:** Whether Iran actually accepts intrusive inspections of its bombed, 60%-enriched-uranium stockpile is the linchpin of the entire ceasefire and determines if the war stays ended.

**Different views:** Jerusalem Post and US officials frame it as Iran conceding to inspections and a $300bn 'peace deal,' while Al Jazeera stresses Iranian denials and 'no new commitments'; Iranian voices (via JPost) cast the MoU as a US 'declaration of defeat,' and the Guardian's commentary frames Trump's victory claims as a self-made catastrophe.
